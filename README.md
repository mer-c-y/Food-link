## 🎯 Our Mission

**FoodLink is a revolutionary web platform designed to tackle two critical problems:**

1. **End Food Waste** - Perfectly good food is discarded daily from events, supermarkets, restaurants, hotels, and food processing companies
2. **Fight Hunger** - Many people in our communities go hungry while edible food is being thrown away

**Our Solution:** Connect generous food providers with hungry people in need, creating a community of compassion that saves food and saves lives.

---

## ❓ The Problem We Solve

### Every Day, Millions of Tons of Food Are Wasted:
- 🏢 **Hotels & Restaurants** - Throw away unsold meals at end of day
- 🏪 **Supermarkets** - Discard slightly cosmetically imperfect but perfectly edible food
- 🎪 **Events** - Excess catering food left over after functions
- 🏭 **Food Factories** - Overproduction and trimmed portions
- 🏠 **Homes** - Leftover meals and prepared food

### Meanwhile, People Go Hungry:
- 😔 Students cannot afford lunch
- 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Families struggle to put food on the table
- 🏚️ Homeless and vulnerable people lack access to nutritious meals
- 🌍 Communities lack food security

- 🤝 Connect with compassionate community members

## 📱 How FoodLink Works (Step-by-Step)

### For Food Providers:

**Step 1: Sign Up**
- Create account (takes 2 minutes)
- Verify your business/location
- Set up your profile

**Step 2: Post Food**
- Click "Share Food"
- Add food type and quantity
- Take a photo
- Set pick-up location and time
- Click "Post"

**Step 3: Impact**
- See people helped
- Read thank you messages
- Build your caring reputation

### For Hungry People:

**Step 1: Browse**
- Open FoodLink
- See food available nearby
- Filter by type or distance

**Step 2: Claim Food**
- Click on food listing
- See details and location
- Get directions
- Confirm you'll collect

**Step 3: Collect**
- Go to location
- Meet provider (safe, respectful)
- Collect your food
- Leave thank you message
