The FoodLink Story

FoodLink was created by Mercy Beryl with a simple belief: No food should be wasted and no one should go hungry.

Every day, millions of tons of perfectly edible food are thrown away from restaurants, hotels, supermarkets, food factories, and homes. At the same time, hungry people struggle to put food on their tables. This waste is both a human tragedy and an environmental disaster.

FoodLink brings these two worlds together. We created a simple, beautiful platform that lets generous food providers easily share their excess food with hungry people who need it. No complicated process. No shame. Just community members helping each other with dignity and respect.

With HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and GitHub Copilot, we built a solution that can be used anywhere, by anyone, for free. Because fighting hunger should be easy and accessible to all.

Together, we can end food waste. Together, we can fight hunger. Together, we can change lives.
